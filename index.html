ROPOSO

  • Net Order After Cancellation: Total Orders × (100 − Cancellation Rate) / 100
  • Delivered Order: Net Orders × (100 − RTO%) / 100
  • RTO Order: Net Orders × RTO%
  • Total Ad Cost: Total Orders × Ad Cost per product
  • Average Ad Cost: Total Ad Cost / Delivered Orders
  • Net RTO Cost: Eligible RTO Orders × ₹72 (Roposo avg RTO charge)
  • Average RTO Cost: Net RTO Cost / Delivered Orders
  • Net Profit/Product: Selling Price − (Avg RTO Cost + Avg Ad Cost + Sourcing Cost)
  • Revenue: Total Orders × Selling Price
  • Net Profit: Delivered Orders × Net Profit/Product
  • Profit %: (Net Profit / Revenue) × 100
  • ROI: Net Profit / Total Ad Cost

E-COMMERCE

  • Net Order After Cancellation: Total Orders × (100 − Cancellation Rate) / 100
  • RTO Orders: Net Orders × RTO%
  • Delivered Orders: Net Orders − RTO Orders
  • Total Freight Charges: Net Orders × Freight Charges
  • Total Ad Cost: Total Orders × Ad Cost
  • Average Ad Cost: Total Ad Cost / Delivered Orders
  • COD Remittance: Delivered Orders × Selling Price
  • Net RTO Cost: RTO Orders × RTO Charges
  • Net Profit/Product: Selling Price − (Avg RTO + Avg Ad + Sourcing + Avg Freight)
  • Net Profit (Real): Net Profit − (RTO Orders × Sourcing Cost)
  • Revenue: Total Orders × Selling Price
  • Profit %: (Net Profit / Revenue) × 100
  • ROI: Net Profit / (Total Freight + Total Ad + Total Sourcing + Net RTO Cost)

GLOWROAD

  • Eligible for RTO Cost: If RTO% ≤ 40% → 0. If RTO% > 40% → Net Orders × (RTO% − 40%)
  • Net RTO Cost: Eligible RTO Cost × ₹70 (Glowroad avg RTO charge)
  • All other formulas same as Roposo
